CNC PLASMA CUTTING AND GAS CUTTER

The plasma cutter is 105A. Plasma for metal cutting is obtained by heating a gas which is conducted through a strong electric field during which the gas is ionized and converted into plasma. Such plasma typically has a temperature of about 22,000 ° C. Plasma-cut metal must have electrical conductivity.

Gas cutting is the process of separating metal by burning it in a stream of oxygen, while simultaneously blowing away the combustion products (slag. In order for the metal to burn, it must be heated to the ignition temperature) combustible gas (acetylene, propane / butane, natural gas…).

Max sheet metal format: 1500 x 3000mm.

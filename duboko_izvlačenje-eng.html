DEEP EXTRACTION SERVICE

Deep drawing is a process of cold processing by which spatial configurations are obtained from sheet metal preparations of circular, square, rectangular or some other shape. It is performed in one or more shaping operations, where the first and subsequent deep drawing operations are essentially different.

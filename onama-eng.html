About the company

Metal-M is a 100% privately owned company, founded in 1996 in Banja Luka. At the beginning of the work, only one item was produced using tools with a hand lever. However, in the upcoming period, there is a procurement of machines that have changed the production program and enabled a variety of products. In the further course of development and expansion of production are the investment and purchase of advanced CNC machines, which we have raised the quality of production to the highest level, especially to satisfy customers.
Today, after 24 years, the company has more than 15 workers, 35 machines and 200 items. In addition to the production of our own items, we also deal with various services. The main activity of the company is the production of metal accessories (barbecue program, fruit growing program, agricultural tools and much more), cutting, stamping, electrostatic powder coating, metal processing on CNC machines and so on..
Thanks to our wide range of products, as well as many successful projects behind us, we are able to offer a wide range of top quality services in the field of metal processing. Thanks to modern technology, such as CNC plasma, milling machine, lathe, punch and a team of professional and professional people, we achieve excellent results in a short period of time.
